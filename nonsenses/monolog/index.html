De Profundis

My mood is like looking at flowers through a mist, vague and not quite right.
Everyone in my heart is like flowing water, with no memory and no emotion.
I know I’ll forget. This is the first and only time I don’t want to forget someone.
So I have engraved you into my memory. In my memory, you have a palace of your own.
I’m not as relaxed as I seem. Sorry.
I don’t want to hurt anyone, but I don’t know what to do.
I know I shouldn’t blame everything on past wounds, but I am filled with guilt and unease. If I have caused you pain, I’ll blame myself.
Countless times I wanted to say, “I’m not that kind of person…” or “I really love you very much. I can give you everything I have.”
But I’m not sure if you still want to listen to me.
I once dreamed of your future, but I don’t know how to make it come true.
Since the day you left, I have been looking back again and again, missing you.
